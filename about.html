About Me

Hi! I'm Aditi, a student living and studying abroad in beautiful Prague. I love exploring new places, discovering hidden cafés, taking photos, and learning about different cultures. When I’m not wandering through cobblestone streets, you’ll probably find me reading, journaling, or planning my next adventure.

My Travel Philosophy

I believe travel is about curiosity, connection, and perspective. It’s less about checking off bucket-list spots and more about slowing down, noticing small details, and embracing spontaneity. My goal is to be present wherever I go — learning, reflecting, and finding beauty in both the familiar and unfamiliar.
