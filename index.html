About Prague

Prague, the capital of the Czech Republic, is a vibrant city in Central Europe known for its stunning architecture, rich history, and charming cobblestone streets. The city beautifully blends Gothic, Baroque, and modern styles, making every corner feel like a step back in time. Czech culture is proud and lively, with a strong tradition in music, literature, and art, and the local cuisine features hearty dishes like goulash, dumplings, and the famous Trdelník pastry. Prague is world-renowned for landmarks such as the Astronomical Clock, Charles Bridge, and Prague Castle, while its scenic river views along the Vltava and the surrounding parks make it a photographer’s dream. Czech is the official language, and while many people speak English, locals are known for being warm, welcoming, and proud of their cultural heritage. Overall, Prague is a city that invites exploration, curiosity, and appreciation for both its historic charm and modern vibrancy.
