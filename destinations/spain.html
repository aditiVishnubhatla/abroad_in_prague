About Spain

Spain is known for its rich history, diverse culture, and lively cities. From the architectural wonders of Barcelona to the flamenco traditions of Andalusia, Spain offers a blend of modern energy and historical charm.

Local Cuisine

Try tapas, paella, churros, jamón ibérico, and regional specialties across the country. Spanish cuisine is famous for its bold flavors and fresh ingredients.

Activities & Attractions

  • Visit Sagrada Familia and Park Güell in Barcelona
  • Explore the Alhambra in Granada
  • Relax on the beaches of Costa del Sol
