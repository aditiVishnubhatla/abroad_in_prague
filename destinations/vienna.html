About Vienna

Vienna is Austria’s capital, known for its classical music, imperial history, and grand architecture. The city boasts museums, palaces, and vibrant coffeehouse culture.

Local Cuisine

Try Wiener Schnitzel, Sachertorte, Apfelstrudel, and sample pastries in traditional Viennese cafés.

Activities & Attractions

  • Visit Schönbrunn Palace and Gardens
  • Explore St. Stephen's Cathedral
  • Attend a classical music concert or opera
